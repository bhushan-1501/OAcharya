  • What Will I Do If The Pandit Is Not Coming On Time?

    Most of the time our Pandits will be before the given time, but sometimes due to some unavoidable circumstances pandits will be a bit late but they will reach your place so you can be assured they will come.

We will not be able to provide any refunds for Pujas cancelled within 48 hrs of your scheduled booking. You can see the detailed cancellation policy.
